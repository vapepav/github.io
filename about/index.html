About VAPEPAV

VAPEPAV stands for VAPE PAVILION.

And YES, as we are the pavilion, your pavilion. Our existance is expected to facilitate Indonesian vapers needs in particular, and International demands in general. We intend to provide relaxation and pleasure, as the same like pavilion does.

VAPEPAV comes from Indonesia. Our main projects are manufacturing high-end vaporizer device, such as mechanical mods, atomizer (RTA/RDA), drip-tips, and accessories stuff. High-end quality doesn't come suddenly, we were having a very very long journey and much hard time of trial and error.

Because of our passion to supply and satisfy your vape needs, so here we are landed and ready to serve you. Supported by a highly qualified CNC machine, both Milling and Turning, makes us feel confident to tell you that we deserve to be counted as your most awaited manufacturer. We believe our patience and thoroughness will become our key to reach success.

We don't produce vape supplies ourself, but we provide premium vape supplies, such as heating wire and organic cotton. We test and sort thousands options that available to meet your high-quality supply demands. Usually we do repackage of a very big quantities to economic quantities, in order to make it affordable supplies.
